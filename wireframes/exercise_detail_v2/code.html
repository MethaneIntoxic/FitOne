Execution Protocol

01

Set the Foundation

Lie flat on the bench. Eyes should be directly under the bar. Plant feet firmly into the floor creating a stable kinetic chain.

02

Grip & Unrack

Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width. Squeeze shoulder blades together to create a solid pressing platform. Unrack with locked elbows.

03

Controlled Descent

Lower the bar slowly to the mid-chest. Keep elbows tucked at a 45-degree angle. Inhale on the way down.
